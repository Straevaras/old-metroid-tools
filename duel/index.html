Rules for a Super Metroid Duel

A Super Metroid Duel involves a race of two people to beat the game of Super Metroid. This race, however, has certain caveats to it determined by each player. Each player will select from a list something to ban and something to be required. All bans and requirements will fall on both players, so players should choose carefully since they will also have to adhere to their own selections. Ensure that any combination of bans and requirements doesn't make the game incompletable. This tool strives to eliminate the impossible comnibations as well as avoiding situtations that require out of bounds.

Ban/Requirement Process

A player will be determined at random to make the first ban or requirement. The player will make only one ban or only one requirement. Once the first player makes the selection, the second player will select both ban and requirement for the race. After the second player has selected both the ban and requirement, the first player will make the remaining selection (a ban if requirement was already selected, or vice versa).

Explaining Bans and Requirements

For bans, players will select either an item or powerup. Items that are banned cannot be picked up or used at all during the course of the entire race. Any powerup that is banned cannot be picked up after picking up the first powerup pack. For missiles, super missiles, and power bombs, a ban will set the maximum allowed at 5 (only one powerup pickup). For energy tanks, a collection of at most 3 energy tanks is allowed. If reserve tanks are banned then no reserve tanks may be picked up at all.

For requirements, players will select either an item, powerup, a last boss, or first mini-boss. Items that are required must be picked up at some point before the game is beaten. Powerups are required with a specific count that must be picked up before beating the game. Requiring a last boss sets which of the four main bosses before Tourian must be killed last. Requiring a first mini-boss first sets which mini-boss must be killed before any major boss can be killed. Other mini-bosses can be killed before the selected one.
